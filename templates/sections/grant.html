JSFoo 2015 GRANT

HasGeek believes in participation from diverse groups at our conferences. We'd like to make every effort possible to facilitate individuals, regardless of their age, sexual orientation, gender and socio-economic backgrounds to attend our conferences. Grants are intended to provide financial assistance to members of these diverse groups, where such members have financial constraints in attending the event. We will make up to 5 grants available for every conference. Actual number of grants will be awarded depending on the quality of applications. Awardees will get to watch some of best speakers from the tech community talk about their craft and cutting edge technology.

Who can apply?

This grant is open to students, professionals (with less than one year of work experience), non-working individuals (individuals on sabbatical, women professionals who have taken a career break) interested in JS and who need financial assistance.

What does this grant include ?

  • Free pass to attend two days of conference and upto to two hands-on workshops

  • Reimbursement of travel and accommodation costs up to 15,000 INR*

    Actual amount granted will be decided on the basis of where the awardee is traveling from to attend the conference and workshops. Travel and accommodation costs will be reimbursed after the conference on submission of expense receipts. Grant awardees are expected to organize their travel and accommodation.

How do I apply?

Apply now
