JSFoo 2015 GRANTS

HasGeek believes in diversity of participation at our conferences. We would like to make every effort to include members from underrepresented groups at our conferences. We have therefore introduced a grants programme for all our upcoming conferences.

Grants are intended to provide financial assistance and/or opportunities to members of these groups and enable them to participate in the community. We will make up to 5 grants available for every conference. Actual number of grants will be awarded depending on the quality of applications. Those who receive the grant will get to watch some of best speakers from the tech community talk about their craft and cutting edge technology.

Who can apply?

Grants are open to

  • Non-working individuals such as women professionals who have dropped out of active coding careers to take sabbaticals either by choice or compulsion
  • Individuals with disabilities
  • Individuals who have financial constraints such as students, people from remote parts of the country, professionals (with less than one year of work experience).

What does this grant include ?

  • Free pass to attend two days of conference and upto to two hands-on workshops

  • Reimbursement of travel and accommodation costs up to 15,000 INR*

    Actual amount granted will be decided on the basis of where the awardee is traveling from to attend the conference and workshops. Travel and accommodation costs will be reimbursed after the conference on submission of expense receipts. Grant awardees are expected to organize their travel and accommodation.

How do I apply?

The applications are reviewed and granted on the basis of information gathered from the form. So, please ensure to include a writeup of your project in Javascript and link to source code of the project.

Apply now

Deadline for submitting applications is 26th August 2015.

For any queries regarding grant, write to us at grants@hasgeek.com
