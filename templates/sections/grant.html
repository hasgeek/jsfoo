at our conferences. We have therefore introduced a grants programme for all our conferences.

Grants are intended to provide financial assistance and/or opportunities to members of these groups and enable them to participate in the community.

We will make up to 5 grants available for every conference. Actual number of grants will be awarded depending on the quality of applications. Awardees will get to watch some of best speakers from the tech community talk about their craft and cutting edge technology.

Who can apply?

Grants are open to students, professionals (with less than one year of work experience), non-working individuals (individuals on sabbatical, women professionals who have taken a career break) and persons with disabilities who are looking for opportunities to participate in the community.

What does this grant include ?

  • Free pass to attend two days of conference and upto to two hands-on workshops

  • Reimbursement of travel and accommodation costs up to 15,000 INR*

    Actual amount granted will be decided on the basis of where the awardee is traveling from to attend the conference and workshops. Travel and accommodation costs will be reimbursed after the conference on submission of expense receipts. Grant awardees are expected to organize their travel and accommodation.

How do I apply?

The applications are reviewed and granted on the basis of information gathered from the form. So, please ensure to include a writeup of your project in Javascript and link to source code of the project.

Apply now
